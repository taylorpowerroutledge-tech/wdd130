About Us - Whitewater Rafting Company

We believe adventure brings people together. We believe in respecting nature and protecting our rivers. We believe safety and teamwork come first. We believe every guest deserves an experience filled with excitement, encouragement, and unforgettable moments. Ride the Rapids. Embrace the Adventure!

History

in 1998 in the rugged river valleys of Nova Scotia, Whitewater Rafting Company began as a small family-owned adventure business with just two rafts and a passion for the outdoors. The company was started by former river guide Mark Sullivan, who wanted to share the excitement and beauty of whitewater rafting with both locals and tourists. In its early years, the company offered weekend rafting tours on nearby rivers and quickly became known for its friendly guides, strong safety standards, and unforgettable experiences. As interest in outdoor adventure tourism grew, Whitewater Rafting Company expanded its services to include kayaking lessons, team-building events, and multi-day wilderness trips. By 2010, the company had grown into one of the region’s leading outdoor adventure providers, welcoming thousands of visitors each year. The business invested in modern equipment, eco-friendly practices, and guide training programs to ensure safe and sustainable adventures for guests of all ages. Today, Whitewater Rafting Company continues to provide exciting river experiences while promoting environmental stewardship and a love for nature. With a reputation for adventure, safety, and community involvement, the company remains a favorite destination for thrill-seekers and outdoor enthusiasts alike.
